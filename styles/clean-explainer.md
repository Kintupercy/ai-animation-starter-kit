# STYLE: Clean Explainer (single-concept lane)

**Use for:** calm, premium explainers of ONE concept — "what is an AI agent", "what is a second brain", "how X actually works". This lane trades speed for polish: fewer cuts, longer holds, and a look that reads expensive.

## Look
- Clean, minimal keyframes; **one idea per frame**; generous negative space.
- Cinematic pacing — slower than trend-style reels; fewer, longer holds (scenes breathe for 4–6s).
- Text is minimal and **baked into the art** (see SKILL.md rule 2). No kinetic captions, no word-per-frame spam.
- Muted, premium palette — **pick 3–4 colors and lock them for every reel in the series.** (Example placeholder: warm off-white background, one deep accent, one soft accent, charcoal for line work. Choose your own — the lock matters more than the colors.)
- Soft push-ins and gentle drifts; motion should feel like a documentary, not a cartoon.

## Image prompt pattern
Build every keyframe prompt as: `<the scene, one idea>` + your locked style suffix, e.g.:
`"minimal editorial illustration, [your bg color], [your accent colors], generous negative space, soft shapes, subtle grain, no text"` — and for text frames, add:
`"integrated into the artwork as clean typography, a headline reads exactly: \"<WORDS>\", crisp and perfectly spelled, part of the composition"`.

## Structure (8–11 beats, ~45–60s)
1. **HOOK** — a relatable question or tension ("Everyone says X. Almost nobody can explain it.")
2–3. Set up the everyday problem the concept solves.
4–7. Unpack the concept step by step — one metaphor per beat, one visual per metaphor.
8–9. The payoff — what changes once you get it.
10. **CTA** — "follow for more" title card, minimal, on-palette.

## Motion
- Animate every frame (image-to-video, ~5s, 9:16 explicit, generated audio OFF).
- Prompt: "all typography stays completely static and crisp; only subtle motion in the background shapes."
- Keep it subtle — this lane should still read as an illustration, not an animation showcase.

## Audio
- Music: one warm, calm instrumental bed (lo-fi, soft piano, or ambient — ~70–100 BPM). Low and constant (~0.12) under the voice, no swells mid-reel, gentle fade at the end.
- No sound effects. Ever, in this lane.
- VO per SKILL.md rule 1 (real voice default, `auto reel:` for the clone).

## Why this lane converts
One concept, fully landed, beats five concepts skimmed. The calm look stands out in a feed full of fast cuts — the pattern interrupt IS the slowness.
